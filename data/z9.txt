Welcome to a northwest facing, beautifully renovated 5-bedroom home. The main floor offers an eat in kitchen with granite countertops, S/S appliances, and new floors. It effortlessly flows into the grand family room, where there is a cozy fireplace and a slider door to the backyard. The yard is gorgeous, making it ideal for entertaining. The family room, formal living & dining rooms have hardwood floors. The open-concept layout of the living area creates an inviting ambiance, perfect for both entertaining & relaxing. Lots of natural light pours in through the home's large windows. There are 3 bedrooms on the main floor, with 1 & a half bathrooms. The second floor offers a primary bedroom with en-suite, an additional bedroom, & a full bath. In the basement, there is plenty of storage, along with the utilities. There is also a well on the property, which is mainly for irrigation. The solar panels are owned by the sellers. Bathroom exhaust has Bluetooth speakers. Has French Drain.
